Taro-Therapy

Taro-Therapy is how I call my approach to holistic healing on subconscious level !

My sessions are a mix of tarot readings and spiritual coaching,
I focus immensely on bringing You value, help You ask the right questions and dive as deeply as possible into what needs more of your awareness in life.

Almost like a therapy... But better!

It is fully welcomed to conversate throughout the reading and share whatever is on your mind. Taro-Therapy session does not only focus on one area or situation you are facing, but the correlation between many areas and even your whole life !

If You're committed enough, You can even treat it as re-scheduled sessions (although my goal is to make You fully independent in your healing!).

All sessions are fully individual and can vary on many levels.
Taro-Therapy is recommended especially for self aware and open minded people, open to new perspectives and approaches to their life and mindset.

Usual time frame of a session is between 1 to 3 hours max.
